Hyresgastforeningen Marmorvagen 13 Website

This project is a website I created for my local tenants' association. It is a practical web project that combines communication, structure, and community service.

In addition to building the website, I am active in the association as vice chairperson and treasurer. That gives me direct insight into the needs of the group and helps me create a site that is useful for members and visitors.

My Role

  • Planned and built the website structure and content.
  • Created a simple web presence for the local association.
  • Combined technical work with responsibility in the organization.
  • Supported clearer communication for members and the local community.
